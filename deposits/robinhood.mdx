How to Deposit from Robinhood

You can use Robinhood Crypto to buy and send USDC to Polymarket. Note that you may have to accept separate terms and verify your identity with Robinhood to activate Robinhood Crypto.

Step 1. Navigate to the Crypto tab 
Step 2. Under ‘Explore more Crypto’, select ‘Tradable’, and ‘USDC’. If you don’t see USDC, it may be in your ‘Holdings’.

Step 3. Buy your desired USDC amount. Want’s entered, select ‘review’, then ‘submit’. 

Step 4. On the USDC page select ‘send’ and Enter the amount you’d like to send (deposit) 

Step 5. Paste your Polymarket deposit address
